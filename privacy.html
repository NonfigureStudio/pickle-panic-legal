Pickle Panic Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pickle Panic, developed by Nonfigure Studio, handles information when using Discord-related features.

Information We Collect

Pickle Panic does not collect, store, sell, or share personal Discord account information on our own servers.

If Discord integration features are used, Discord may provide limited information required for features such as game activity, Rich Presence, or related Discord functionality. This may include basic Discord account identifiers or activity status handled through Discord's own services.

How We Use Information

Any Discord-related information is used only to enable Discord integration features such as showing that the player is playing Pickle Panic or supporting Discord-related game features.

Data Storage

Pickle Panic does not store Discord user data on our own servers.

Third-Party Services

Pickle Panic may use third-party services such as Discord and Steam. These services have their own privacy policies and terms.

Children's Privacy

Pickle Panic does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
